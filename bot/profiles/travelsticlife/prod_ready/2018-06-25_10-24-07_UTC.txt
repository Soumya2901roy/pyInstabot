@patisseriebyfranziska is a new French pastrie shop opened by a German lady chef in Russell street opposite Indian hobby centre but she started 2 years back in Kolkata as home Baker. She import most of her ingredients and she use only fresh stuff.
We highly recommend everyone to try their blueberry vanilla tart and chocolate eclair
They are open 11 Am - 11pm Tuesday till Sunday, Monday close
